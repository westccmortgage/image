Privacy Policy

Wallet WCCM · AI Mortgage Strategy Advisor — operated by West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This notice describes how the Wallet WCCM AI Mortgage Strategy Advisor (the “Advisor”) handles information. Using the Advisor is for educational and planning purposes only; it is not a mortgage application, a Loan Estimate, an approval, or a commitment to lend.

Who we are

The Advisor is operated by West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc. (CA DRE Corporation License #02440065 · NMLS #2817729), with licensed broker Anatoliy Kanevsky (CA DRE Broker License #01385024 · NMLS #2775380). Corporate site: westccmortgage.com.

What we collect

  • Scenario details you type (e.g. purchase price, down payment, occupancy, employment type, ZIP/county, goals). These describe your situation so the Advisor can compare loan paths.
  • Contact details you choose to submit for broker review (name, phone, email, preferred contact time and language).
  • Documents you choose to upload for licensed broker review.
  • Basic, non-identifying usage events (e.g. which stage of the conversation you reached), used to improve the experience.

Please do not enter full Social Security numbers, dates of birth, or full bank-account numbers unless a licensed mortgage professional specifically requests them through a secure channel.

How your information is stored

  • Your active scenario lives only in your browser during the session; it is not saved across browser sessions, and sensitive financial details are not stored in local storage.
  • A non-sensitive language preference may be stored locally to remember your choice.
  • Documents submitted for broker review are stored privately on the server and shared only through protected, access-controlled links.

When we share information

We only forward your information when you explicitly submit it for broker review. In that case it is delivered to our authorized customer-relationship system and to a licensed mortgage broker at West Coast Capital Mortgage Inc. for follow-up. We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers

  • Hosting — the site is hosted on a cloud hosting provider.
  • AI responses — your typed messages are processed by a third-party AI model provider to generate advisor replies. Deterministic financial figures are calculated by our own tools, not invented by the model.
  • CRM / delivery — broker-review submissions are delivered to our authorized CRM.

Cookies & tracking

The Advisor uses minimal browser storage needed to operate (such as your theme and language preference). It does not require advertising cookies to function.

Your choices

  • You can decline to provide contact information and still use the Advisor.
  • You can clear your session at any time with “Start over”.
  • To request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you submitted for broker review, contact us at (310) 686-5053.
